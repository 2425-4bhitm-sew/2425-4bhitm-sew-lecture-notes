What is MPG?

MPG is the abbreviation of miles per gallon which is the distance traveled in a vehicle with one gallon of gasoline or diesel fuel. Mpg value tells us how many miles we can travel with 1 gallon of fuel with a car. MPG shows the fuel economy of a car.

How to Convert Miles per Gallon (US) to l/100km

In order to convert mpg to l/100km, we need to first convert gallon (US) to litres, and convert miles to kilometers.

Kilometers miles conversion factor:

1 mile = 1.609344 kilometers
1 mi = 1.609344 km

US gallon litre conversion factor:

1 US gallon = 3.78541178 litres
1 gal = 3.78541178 l

Mpg in fuel consumption units:

mpg = Miles per Gallon
mpg = miles / gal

putting kilometers equivalent in mpg equation
mpg = 1.609344 km / gal

putting litres equivalent in mpg equation
mpg = 1.609344 km / 3.78541178 l

let's divide 1 by our equation
because we have miles/gal, but we need to find l/100km

3.78541178 l / 1.609344 km
2.35214583 l / km

we need to find litres per 100 kilometers,
so we have multiply and divide by 100 of the right hand side

235.214583 l / 100km

in order to find litres per 100 kilometers
we need to divide mpg by 235.214583

l/100km = 235.214583 / mpg
